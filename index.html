Occupation Matchmaker

This website shows a visualization representing the frequency of people in certain occupations marrying people in the same of other occupations. There is a search bar in which you can search for an occupation and see which other occupations they typically marry. This is shown in bubbles/circles which appear as different sizes according to the frequency of marriages between the bubble/circle's occupation and the occupation you searched for. When you search for a new occupation, the bubbles transition to the next size, which can be done with the .trasnition() feature of d3, rather than simply jumbing from one sized circle to the next from one instant to the next.
